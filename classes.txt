Data structure in the table

dictionary, keys: numbers,
values: list with 2 string members: field spec. piece e.g. A1, A2, A3
missing: start fields, house

Data structure in player

int: how many is in the start field 0-4
During the play:
list: fields where the pieces stand on (0-4: int)

House:
dictionary: 4 places, on wich the pieces can stand
